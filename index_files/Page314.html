- No Cheating or Plagiarism

All academic work must be honest and original. Cheating in exams, copying

assignments, or using someone else’s work without proper acknowledgment is considered academic misconduct. Students caught cheating or plagiarizing may face disciplinary action, including a failing grade or suspension.


- Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking Policy

The possession, use, or distribution of alcohol, illegal drugs, or any form of substance abuse is strictly prohibited on campus. Smoking is also not allowed in any college building or public area. Violation of this policy may result in immediate disciplinary action, including suspension or

expulsion.
