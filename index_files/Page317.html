6. Campus Rules & Discipline

- Rules for dormitories

Quiet Hours:

Residents must observe quiet hours from 9:00 PM to 6:00 AM to allow

everyone a peaceful environment for studying and sleeping. Loud music, shouting, or disturbances during these hours are not allowed.

 

Cleanliness:

Each student is responsible for **keeping their room and shared areas clean** and tidy. Inspections may be carried out, and failure to maintain cleanliness can result in a warning or penalties.

 

Visitors Policy:

Visitors are only allowed during designated hours and must sign in at the dormitory office. Overnight guests are strictly prohibited unless prior permission is granted by the dormitory supervisor.
