6. Participation in College Events

Attendance at official college events (e.g. assemblies,

workshops, seminars) is compulsory unless otherwise stated.

These events may be counted as part of class attendance.

 

5. Code of Conduct

- Dress Code

Proper upkeep and decent dressing are required of all students during

classes and official hours.

- Respect for Others

Students must treat fellow students, staff, and visitors with courtesy,

kindness, and fairness at all times. Discrimination, bullying, harassment, or any form of verbal or physical abuse will not be tolerated. Everyone has the right to feel safe and respected on campus.
