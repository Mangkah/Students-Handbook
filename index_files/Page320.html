7. Student Services

- Computer Lab

The Computer Lab is available to students for research, assignments, and practical work related to their courses. Access is only permitted during scheduled hours or with approval from the instructor or lab assistant. Students must not install or delete any software, and must use the

computers responsibly and respectfully. Any form of misuse may lead to loss of access privileges.
