- Attendance Requirements

1. Minimum Attendance

Students must attend at least 80% of all scheduled classes,

tutorials, and practical sessions for each course.

Falling below the 80% requirement may result in

disqualification from sitting for final exams.

2. Attendance Records

Lecturers and instructors will keep daily attendance records.

It is the student’s responsibility to sign the attendance register or respond to roll call.

3. Absenteeism

Absences must be justified with valid reasons (e.g. medical

certificate, family emergency).

Students must inform their class coordinator or lecturer in

advance if they expect to miss a class.

4. Consequences of Poor Attendance

Warning Letter after repeated unexcused absences.

Parental Notification.

Suspension or course failure if poor attendance continues.

5. Late Arrival and Early Departure

Students arriving 15 minutes or more late may be marked as absent.

Leaving class without permission is considered a disciplinary offense.
